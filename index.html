The Institute of Management and Information Technology(IMIT), formerly known as College of Accountancy and Management Studies
(CAMS), Cuttack was founded way back in 1962. It is a unique prestigious institution of the Government of Odisha, and has positioned
itself as a premier institute offering various professional and technical courses that groom the students to cater to the expectations
